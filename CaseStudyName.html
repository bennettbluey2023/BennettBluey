How is technology used (Prompt #2)

Most Effective strategies

Technology is used poorly in regard to mental health however, as many athletes face trolls, slander, and hate online due to poor performance on the field which can then negatively affect psychological feelings off the field.

Athletes and Professional Team are given a psychologist on nearly every pro sports team that is able to sit down with athletes and work with them 1 on 1

Implication - We should encourage athletes to maintain playing their sports by reminding them of the psychological and physical benefits that they get from competing.

Explanation - If athletes are reminded of the positives they feel when competing, they may feel more pure joy when competing and lift overall mental health.

Implication - We can look closer at retired athletes and study ways that they can stay involved and implemented in their sports so they retain some of the psychological benefits of being around a sport that they felt from playing their sport.

Explanation - Retired athletes that are older will still hold on to the joy they once felt from playing their sport. This will increase the mental health and happiness older folks feel and relieve depression of their loss of playing their sport.
